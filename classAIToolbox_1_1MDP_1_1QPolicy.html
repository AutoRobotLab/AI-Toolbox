Detailed Description

This class models a policy through a QFunction.

This function provides a way to sample actions without the need to compute a full Policy from a QFunction. This is useful because often many methods need to modify small parts of a Qfunction for progressive improvement, and computing a full Policy at each step can become too expensive to do.
